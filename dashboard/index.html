KEY BUSINESS INSIGHTS
⚠️
Month-to-Month Contracts Are Critical
Customers on month-to-month contracts are 5x more likely to churn compared to those on two-year contracts, indicating a clear retention opportunity.
CHURN RATE: 42% vs 8%
💸
High Charges Drive Churn
Churned customers pay on average $74.44/month vs $61.27 for retained customers. Price sensitivity is a major factor, especially in the first year.
$13.17 MONTHLY DELTA
📅
Critical Retention Window: 0–12 Months
63% of all churn occurs within the first 12 months. Early onboarding engagement and loyalty programs should be prioritized in this window.
63% OF CHURN IN YEAR 1
🌐
Fiber Optic Users Show High Churn
Despite being a premium service, fiber optic customers have a 41.9% churn rate — suggesting service quality issues or unmet expectations.
41.9% FIBER CHURN RATE
🛡️
Tech Support Reduces Churn
Customers without tech support churn at 41.6% vs 15.2% for those with it. Bundling support services could significantly reduce churn risk.
2.7X LOWER CHURN WITH SUPPORT
💳
Electronic Check = Churn Signal
Customers paying via electronic check have the highest churn rate at 45.3%. This payment method may correlate with lower commitment or engagement.
45.3% ELECTRONIC CHECK CHURN
